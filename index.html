Beatrice Chebet breaks women's world 5km record in Barcelona

The 24-year-old Kenyan finished the race in 13 minutes and 54 seconds to become the first woman to break the 14-minute barrier in the event. Chebet, who won 5,000m and 10,000m gold at the Paris Olympics earlier this year, beat her previous mark - set a year ago to the day in the same race - by 19 seconds. "I'm super happy as everything went according to plan," she said. "I felt capable of running under 14 minutes and I managed to do so. Two races in Barcelona and two world records, can I ask for more? "My focus for next year is to win gold medals over 5,000m and 10,000m at the World Championships in Tokyo."
